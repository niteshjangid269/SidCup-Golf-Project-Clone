ABOUT US

Home to a 46-bay, multi-tier, floodlit driving range, powered by Toptracer Range technology. Complimented by a practice green and bunker, coffee shop and American Golf Store.
There truly is something for everyone as we also boast two outdoor 18-hole dinosaur themed crazy golf courses.
Please note: we are a cashless venue. The range closes at 10pm with last balls at 9pm.

TOPTRACER RANGE

For the true golf geek, Toptracer provides shot stats like ball speed, apex, curve, carry and more. Our shot traces can be integrated into virtual course graphics to animate the ball's trajectory in a side-slab format.

ADVENTURE GOLF

Adventure golf is similar to miniature golf when scoring. You'll want as few strokes as possible at the end of the 18-hole course to be declared the winner.
